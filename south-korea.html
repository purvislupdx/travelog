I spent spring break of 2016 in Seoul, South Korea. Even though it was a whirlwind visit, I got to see a lot of the city - between the food and people, it was an amazing experience.

One of the great sights was the Ewha Women's University - the centerpiece of the campus is a huge underground complex of classes and offices accessed by a sunken passage that resembles a gorge.
