England

My dad is originally from England. I've visited a couple of times and always enjoy the time we've spent in London. It's a place filled with history, tradition and so-so food. London is a great city for wandering - there's so much to see in out of the way places and if you run out of steam, there's always a bus or tube train nearby to get you back to base.
